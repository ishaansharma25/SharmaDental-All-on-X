Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes, including:

  • Authentication: To recognize you when you visit our website and remember your preferences
  • Security: To protect user accounts and our website
  • Analytics: To analyze how visitors use our website and monitor website performance
  • Preferences: To remember information about your browser and preferences

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use both session cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your device until you delete them).

4. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, if you limit the ability of websites to set cookies, you may worsen your overall user experience.
