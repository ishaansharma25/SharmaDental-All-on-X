About All-on-X Implants

The All-on-X technique was developed as the optimal solution for restoring full arches of upper and lower jaws. This revolutionary approach provides patients with a fixed, non-removable dental arch secured by just four strategically placed dental implants - all completed in a single day. Perfect for patients who currently have dentures or need complete smile makeovers.

Key Benefits

  • Full arch restoration with only 4-6 implants per jaw
  • Effective even with significant bone loss (no grafting needed in most cases)
  • Walk out with teeth the same day - leave smiling!
  • Faster recovery compared to traditional implants
  • More affordable than individual implants for full arch restoration
